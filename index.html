Notes:

  1. To get your GitHub token, log into your account and go to Settings -> Personal access tokens.
  2. Get your Trello app key. (You must be logged into you Trello account.)
  3. There is a limitation in Trello where comment attribution from another system cannot be carried over. All comments imported into Trello must be attributed to whomever is logged in (i.e. running this script) at the time.
